Alcohol 3D Illustration

As an Illustrator I developed ideas primarily for a single still frame and leveraged visuall puns to make the most out of a single viewer interaction. As began working with 3D more I initially approached it with the same practice. In this illustration I made for an article on alcoholism, I wanted to evoke the concept of helplessness and isolation.
