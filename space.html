Space

Graduate Motion Media Project

Space was one of the last projects I completed as a student at SCAD. Stylistically I had been trending towards a screen printed look to my illustrations and had been working almost exclusively in terms of limited colors that could be screen printed. With this project I wanted to see if it was possible to bring that style to animation.

I used cel shaders in Cinema 4D and post process treatments in After effects to create the screen print look.
