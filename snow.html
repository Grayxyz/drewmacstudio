Snow

Snow

One of my first experiments with real-time rendering, prior to the advent of RTX technology. This scene was designed to be rendered without global illumination or subsurface scattering. Instead emission and albedo maps were baked to fake subsurface scattering and more complex lighting systems were built to approximate global illumination

With this project I also began experimenting with procedrualism in the ligthing around the house. I also started using displacement maps in the footprints to speed up the modeling process and facilitate animation
