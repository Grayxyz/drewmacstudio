Tortoise

Tortoise Character

This creature began as a concept scketch in Z-Brush and just kind of took off. I was in the process of transitioning from traditional brute force and unbiased rendering to real-time rendering in Unreal Engine. I just kept adding to the model as I became familar with various maps and baking processs

Process

The sketch in Zbrush utilized z spheres for the basic shape. From there I added more shapes for the shell segments and sculpted them out before fusing them together. The retopology was completed in Modo and from there I took the model into Substance painter to add texture and albedo.
