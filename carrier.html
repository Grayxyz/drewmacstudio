Carrier Ship

Carrier

The carrier model was the last model I created in Maya before transitioning fully to Houdini. The model was initially developed as an asset for a friend's mobile game as a donation. With this model I drew inspiration from the District 9 concept art and expolored the use of geometric designs created in Illustrator as decorative markings in 3D. After creating the patterns, panting the metalic textures and greebles, I applied the paint patterns with curvature maps and ambient occlusion maps to simulate wear.
